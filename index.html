Telegram Bot for Kazakhstan National Anti-Doping Committee
Software Engineer

Kazakhstan National Anti-Doping Committee, Almaty, Kazakhstan

KazNADC addressed challenges with tracking their athletes, who are committing some unfair acts in sports. The Committee does not have any professional tools to gather information about athletes and athletes do not have any tools to provide feedback, either. The athletes are supposed to send information about their food intake, any treatment or substances use, periodically.

Consequently, KazNADC ban those athletes, who stop providing any information and as a result, they cannot participate in Olympic Games. Moreover, not many athletes are acquainted with the list of forbidden substances, so they suffer from KazNADC’s punishment actions. My bot solves these problems and contributes to the fairness in sports.
