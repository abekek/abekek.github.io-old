My Impossible List
What is this?

An Impossible List - is a list which contains my goals from different spheres of my life. It helps me to track my progress and motivates me to act. Some of my monthly goals I post on my Telegram channel - Abekek Notes.

The idea of creating the impossible list came to me from Thomas Frank.

Just a quote to motivate myself (it's in Russian, but I translated it):

In a year you will regret not starting today.
